About Us

The Rosewood Public Library was started in 2020, to take our love of reading to the next level. We have collected pre-owned and pre-loved books from our friends and family all over the country for the last five years for this venture. We invite all book lovers and book readers to join us on our journey.

Do drop by to read something new today! Kindly follow all COVID protocols like wearing masks at all times, maintaining social distancing and carrying your hand sanitisers.
